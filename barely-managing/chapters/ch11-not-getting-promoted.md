# Chapter 11: Telling Someone They're Not Getting Promoted

Draft status: scaffold complete.

## Chapter intent
Deliver non-promotion decisions clearly while preserving dignity and motivation.

## Core anecdote
A promotion decision made too early creates multi-year trust debt.

## Key sections
- Communicating no versus not-yet.
- Separating value from level readiness.
- Building growth contracts.

## Explicit Four Bases map
- Team: fairness signal for peers.
- Role: criteria not yet met.
- Business: role timing and need.
- Legal/Ethical: consistent standards.

## Concrete managerial decision
Hold promotion until evidence reaches consistent level bar.

## Manager action checklist
- State decision clearly.
- Tie gaps to observable criteria.
- Set reassessment timeline.
