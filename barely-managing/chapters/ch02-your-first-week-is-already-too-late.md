# Chapter 2: Your First Week Is Already Too Late

Draft status: scaffold complete.

## Chapter intent
Teach first-week triage and listening patterns that prevent avoidable trust failures.

## Core anecdote
Inherited delivery risk appears before the new manager has baseline context.

## Key sections
- First-week anti-patterns.
- Listening sprint and risk mapping.
- Week-two communication plan.

## Concrete managerial decision
Pause a noncritical deliverable to stabilize staffing and accountability.

## Manager action checklist
- Run 1:1 listening interviews.
- Publish a risk map with owners.
- Confirm top assumptions before changing process.
