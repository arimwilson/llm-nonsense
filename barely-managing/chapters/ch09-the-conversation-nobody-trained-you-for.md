# Chapter 9: The Conversation Nobody Trained You For

Draft status: scaffold complete.

## Chapter intent
Prepare managers for first high-stakes performance conversations.

## Core anecdote
A manager runs a difficult conversation without a reliable preparation model.

## Key sections
- Signs the conversation is overdue.
- Hard conversation pre-brief structure.
- Follow-up checkpoints and support plans.

## Explicit Four Bases map
- Team: burden transfer and trust erosion.
- Role: expectation gap and required behaviors.
- Business: delivery impact and risk.
- Legal/Ethical: factual documentation and process alignment.

## Concrete managerial decision
Move from informal coaching to formal improvement plan.

## Manager action checklist
- Prepare one sentence per base.
- Align with HR before conversation.
- Document outcomes and next steps.
