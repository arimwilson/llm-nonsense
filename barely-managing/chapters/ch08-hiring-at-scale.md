# Chapter 8: Hiring at Scale When You're Not the Bottleneck

Draft status: scaffold complete.

## Chapter intent
Design hiring systems where quality scales without manager heroics.

## Core anecdote
Candidate throughput stalls because approvals are manager-centric.

## Key sections
- Defining interview signal quality.
- Debrief calibration standards.
- Ownership and SLAs for decisions.

## Concrete managerial decision
Delegate debrief facilitation to trained panel leads.

## Manager action checklist
- Certify interviewers.
- Set debrief turnaround standards.
- Monitor conversion and quality metrics.
