# Chapter 5: The 1:1 Is the Job

Draft status: scaffold complete.

## Chapter intent
Show how 1:1 quality drives trust, performance clarity, and retention.

## Core anecdote
A high performer disengages after months of status-only meetings.

## Key sections
- Why 1:1s are the operating system of management.
- Report-owned agendas.
- Follow-through systems for commitments.

## Concrete managerial decision
Redesign all 1:1s around development, decisions, and blockers.

## Manager action checklist
- Give reports agenda ownership.
- Track commitments with dates.
- Audit patterns monthly.
