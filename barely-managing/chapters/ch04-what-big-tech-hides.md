# Chapter 4: What Big Tech Teaches You (And What It Hides)

Draft status: scaffold complete.

## Chapter intent
Identify which management habits are portable and which depend on mature company systems.

## Core anecdote
A preventable manager error is absorbed by strong process scaffolding.

## Key sections
- Support systems that mask weak judgment.
- Portability audit of management habits.
- Rebuilding missing safety nets.

## Concrete managerial decision
Create explicit replacement processes after moving to lower-process environments.

## Manager action checklist
- List systems currently covering your mistakes.
- Mark each as portable or non-portable.
- Build replacements for non-portable systems.
