# Chapter 1: The Offer You Can't Refuse (Or Can You?)

Draft status: scaffold complete.

## Chapter intent
Help new managers decide whether to accept a first management role for the right reasons.

## Core anecdote
A high-pressure management offer arrives before role expectations are clarified.

## Key sections
- The hidden tradeoffs of moving from IC to manager.
- Questions to ask before saying yes.
- Setting explicit success criteria with your manager.

## Concrete managerial decision
Accept the role only after defining first-90-day expectations and support.

## Manager action checklist
- List your motivations and non-negotiables.
- Confirm scope, support, and success metrics.
- Document why this role is right now.
