# Chapter 3: The Identity Crisis Nobody Warns You About

Draft status: scaffold complete.

## Chapter intent
Reframe manager credibility away from technical dominance and toward decision quality.

## Core anecdote
A senior engineer challenges a new manager's authority in public.

## Key sections
- Letting go of maker identity.
- Credibility signals for non-expert managers.
- Coaching senior ICs fairly.

## Concrete managerial decision
Delegate technical design ownership while retaining escalation accountability.

## Manager action checklist
- Clarify decision rights.
- Ask for dissent explicitly.
- Track team outcomes over personal output.
