# Chapter 16: Technical Decisions When You're Not the Expert Anymore

Draft status: scaffold complete.

## Chapter intent
Improve technical decision quality through process design.

## Core anecdote
Two senior leads present conflicting architecture proposals under deadline pressure.

## Key sections
- Decision rights and dissent structure.
- Reversible versus irreversible calls.
- Review triggers and rollback criteria.

## Concrete managerial decision
Choose the reversible option with explicit review gates.

## Manager action checklist
- Clarify decision owner.
- Capture assumptions and tradeoffs.
- Define rollback conditions.
