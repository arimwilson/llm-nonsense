# Chapter 6: Feedback That Doesn't Require HR Intervention

Draft status: scaffold complete.

## Chapter intent
Enable managers to deliver specific feedback early enough to prevent formal escalation.

## Core anecdote
A vague feedback pattern ends in surprise escalation.

## Key sections
- Why vague feedback is unfair.
- SBI+Stakes+Next Date model.
- Documentation and follow-up cadence.

## Concrete managerial decision
Deliver direct feedback with examples before process escalation becomes mandatory.

## Manager action checklist
- Use behavior-based examples.
- Name impact and required change.
- Schedule follow-up checkpoint.
