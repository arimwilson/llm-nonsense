# Chapter 17: Building Culture That Survives You

Draft status: scaffold complete.

## Chapter intent
Make culture durable through codified behaviors and manager systems.

## Core anecdote
Collaboration quality drops when a small set of leaders is absent.

## Key sections
- Turning values into observable behaviors.
- Reinforcement loops for managers.
- Measuring culture without vanity metrics.

## Concrete managerial decision
Operationalize three non-negotiable cultural behaviors with review metrics.

## Manager action checklist
- Codify expected behaviors.
- Train managers to reinforce them.
- Audit culture outcomes by team and region.
