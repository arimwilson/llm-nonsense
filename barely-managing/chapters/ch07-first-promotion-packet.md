# Chapter 7: Your First Promotion Packet

Draft status: scaffold complete.

## Chapter intent
Teach promotion advocacy that is fair, evidence-based, and calibration-ready.

## Core anecdote
A promotion case requires 47 drafts before acceptance.

## Key sections
- Promotion evidence versus narrative argument.
- Calibration expectations.
- Communicating not-yet decisions.

## Concrete managerial decision
Delay submission one cycle to align evidence with level standards.

## Manager action checklist
- Map proof to each level criterion.
- Gather cross-functional corroboration.
- Rehearse panel objections.
